Audovia is a music making application for classical musicians.  Up to fifteen instrumental voices and a percussion track can be used.

← click on one of the samples to play or pause and double-click to rewind.

    

Audovia is great for making background music for videos or backing tracks for playing or singing along to.  Even ringtones for your mobile phone.

The quality of your music will depend on your choice of instrument for each voice and your choice of soundbank.
