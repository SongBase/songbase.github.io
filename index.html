Make Music with Audovia
 
Audovia 3.1.0 for Microsoft Windows 
Transcribe your scores into Audovia using JFugue MusicString notation.  Songs can have up to 15 instrumental voices and a percussion track with instruments chosen from a .sf2 or .dls soundbank.  Play back your music and export to MIDI, MusicXML or WAV.  The WAV files can be opened in Audacity and exported to MP3.
